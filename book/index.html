Our meeting point

Place to be: Grauwwerkersstraat 29, 8000 Bruges

Start from the Market, follow Vlamingstraat and turn to the left into Grauwwerkersstraat. It's a 5 minute walk.

Parking information

Nearest car parking is underground parking 'Biekorf'

Main entrance address: Naaldenstraat 8, 8000 Bruges. The public parking is 1 minute walking from our place.

Alternative parking option:

Car parking next to the train station, called 'Interparking Centrum Station'

The public parking is at 20 minutes walking from our place.

Address: Buiten Begijnenvest (or also Chantrellstraat), 8000 Bruges.
