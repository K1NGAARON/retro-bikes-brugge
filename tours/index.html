Are you ready to experience Bruges?

We start from the imposing monastery garden of Hof Bladelin where you are immediately immersed in Bruges' rich history.

Experience medieval drama during a unique 2.5-hour tour of Bruges' cobblestones with an authentic local.
You will visit the highlights and unknown places that no other guide can offer you.

Enter a real medieval city palace and discover the Silent Bruges, a secluded neighbourhood where not many tourists come.
Feel, taste and enjoy our wonderful city and get ultimate tips & tricks for your further stay.
